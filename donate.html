Services Provided for Madhumitra

Madhumitra, a 10-year-old child diagnosed with acute renal failure, was supported through our specialized services. Our team facilitated essential diagnostic tests and medical interventions under pediatric care to aid in her recovery.

Medical Services Provided to Madhumitra

With your generous support, Madhumitra received critical pediatric care through our services such as:

  • Advanced diagnostic tests including ADAMTS13 and Whole Exome Sequencing
  • Specialist consultations and follow-up care
  • Dialysis support for acute renal failure
  • Coordination with Lal Path Labs for reduced testing costs

These services are part of what we regularly offer to children in need. Your donation helps us continue this mission.
