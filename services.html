Hope for Madhumitra: Life-Saving Tests Funded Just in Time

Madhumitra, a bright 10-year-old girl from Netaji Street, Samarayapatti, was recently diagnosed with acute renal failure and placed on emergency dialysis. Despite intensive treatment, her condition worsened, and a kidney biopsy revealed a rare disease called thrombotic microangiopathy.

Her doctors urgently recommended two advanced diagnostic tests to guide further treatment: ADAMTS13 levels and Whole Exome Sequencing. These tests are crucial for identifying the exact cause and creating a personalized treatment plan — but they come at a high cost and are only available in specialized labs like Lal Path Labs.

Sadly, Madhumitra’s father had passed away, and her mother, Lalita, who is her sole caregiver, had already exhausted their limited resources on dialysis and intensive care. The cost of these tests — over ₹30,000 — was simply out of reach.

With nowhere else to turn, Lalita approached Abinayaa Child Heart Foundation Trust for help. Recognizing the urgency, the Trust acted immediately. Thanks to their swift support and a generous subsidy from Lal Path Labs, the total cost was reduced to ₹25,920.

In August, the Trust transferred the amount directly to the lab, and the tests were successfully conducted — all without placing any further financial burden on the family. These critical diagnostics have given doctors the clarity they need and brought hope back to Madhumitra's life.

Your support can make a life-saving difference — just like it did for Madhumitra.
